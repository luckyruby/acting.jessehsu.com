About Me

Jesse began his career in New York where he studied with UCB and after moving to Los Angeles a few years ago, he has booked 12 commercials from 2020-2022 and 10 in 2024. He has taken commercial technique courses with AHC, Killian's and voiceover classes at Real Voice LA and has a commercial voice demo.


In 2022, he was a guest star in a web series, Millie & Maddie and also starred in his first feature film, "Starring Jerry As Himself" which premiered at the 2022 Slamdance Film Festival and is now available to stream on Amazon Prime Video and Apple TV+.
